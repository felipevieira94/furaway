Strategy & CRO Notes

🎯 OTO Conversion Tips

  • The car upsell works because it's a genuinely different use case — not just "buy more of the same"
  • Decline button copy: "No thanks — I don't need it for the car" — makes declining feel like a concession, not just a button press
  • Timer is real — set to 15 minutes in Shopify post-purchase. Creates urgency without dishonesty
  • Target AOV lift: £11.99 add-on on a £34.99 order = 34% AOV increase on converting customers
  • Test a second OTO: a set of microfibre cloths or a pet hair travel kit at £8.99

📧 Email Flow Metrics

  • Confirmation email (Email 1): target 65–75% open rate — transactional emails have the highest open rate of any sequence
  • Dispatch email (Email 2): target 55–65% open rate — customers are actively waiting for their order
  • Review request (Email 3): target 35–45% open rate · 8–15% click rate · 20–30% review completion
  • Re-order email (Email 4): target 25–35% open rate · 4–8% click rate
  • All four emails should be set as "transactional" in Klaviyo to bypass marketing unsubscribes

🛠 Shopify Implementation

  • Thank You Page customisation: use ReConvert or Zipify Pages for full post-purchase page control
  • OTO (post-purchase upsell): AfterSell or Zipify OCU — both integrate natively with Shopify checkout
  • Email flow: Klaviyo — create a "Fulfilled Order" flow for dispatch email, "Placed Order" for confirmation
  • Review collection: Judge.me or Okendo — both auto-trigger review request emails on schedule
  • Referral: ReferralCandy or Smile.io — embed referral widget into Thank You Page

💰 Revenue Impact Estimates

  • OTO conversion rate (cold traffic): typically 8–18% of buyers accept a post-purchase upsell
  • At 12% OTO uptake on 100 orders/month: +£143.88 additional revenue, zero extra ad spend
  • Review emails generating 25 reviews/month compound into significantly lower CAC over time (social proof flywheel)
  • Referral programme at 5% referral rate: every 20 customers generates 1 new customer for near-zero cost
  • Combined: post-purchase sequence can add 15–25% to total monthly revenue with no additional ad spend
