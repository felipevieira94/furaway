Got questions?

We thought you might

How is FurAway™ different from a regular lint roller?
A lint roller uses disposable adhesive sheets that run out quickly and end up in the bin. FurAway™ uses a reusable surface that lasts for years, rinses clean under water, and never needs replacing. It's also significantly more effective on deeply embedded pet hair.
Does it work for both cats and dogs?
Yes — FurAway™ works equally well on cat and dog hair, regardless of coat type. Whether your pet has short, fine hair or long, thick fur, the bidirectional surface picks it up effectively.
What surfaces can I use it on?
FurAway™ is suitable for sofas, upholstered chairs, car seats, clothing (including knitwear), bedding, carpets, rugs, pet beds, and most fabric surfaces. If in doubt, test a small inconspicuous area first.
Will it damage delicate fabrics?
FurAway™ has been designed to be gentle on fabrics while still being effective. It works safely on cotton, linen, polyester, velvet, and most blends. We recommend a gentle stroke on delicate fabrics such as silk.
How do I clean it?
Simply rinse under cold running water for 10–15 seconds. The collected fur releases easily. Allow to air dry before storing. No detergent or special cleaning required.
How long does it last?
With proper care, FurAway™ lasts for years. The surface retains its performance with regular rinsing. Unlike disposable alternatives, there's no degradation over time from normal use.
When will my order arrive?
We dispatch all UK orders within 1–2 working days. Standard delivery typically takes 3–5 working days. Express delivery options are available at checkout.
What if it doesn't work for me?
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy for any reason, simply get in touch and we'll arrange a full refund — no questions asked, no return postage required.
Is it eco-friendly?
Yes. Because FurAway™ is fully reusable and produces no disposable waste, it's significantly kinder to the environment than lint rollers. One FurAway™ replaces hundreds of disposable lint roller sheets.
Can I use it on my pet directly?
FurAway™ is designed for use on fabric surfaces and clothing, not for grooming your pet directly. For grooming, we'd recommend a dedicated pet brush.
Do I need to buy one per room?
Many customers do keep a FurAway™ in multiple locations for convenience — by the sofa, in the bedroom, and one near the front door. They're compact enough to tuck into any drawer, and our multi-pack options make this great value.
Is it safe to use around children?
Absolutely. FurAway™ contains no chemicals, adhesives, or electrical components. It's completely safe to use and store in family homes.
